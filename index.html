A New Beginning Quest Log

Not every adventure ends victorious… But this begins with a choice. Will you join?

Hello fellow Adventurers,

We, Namratha Holla and Shreyas Hebbar, are inviting you to journey on a quest to ne’er before seen lands on a journey I had not foreseen. And we need your help to see it through!

Not as a Hero, a Warrior or even a Champion!

Not as a Mage, a Wizard or a Sage!

I just need your soul

I just need your presence on this journey!

The Path is not simple, it winds through deep forests, down sloping ghats, past endless beaches.

You will be asked to walk through trees, dance under the stars, and to gather around the embers of tradition.

And yes, you will be fed scrumptiously along the way.

So will you help us slay this final dragon called Marriage!

This quest will take you across 3 different cities, forging magical armor in the first step on December 4th, sharpening your mind and body in the second before slaying the dreaded dragon over the embers on December 5th and 6th and finally enjoying the fruits of our conquest in the third on December 9th

To find out more stay tuned for the next exciting episode of Dragon Ball Z!!!

Quest 1

Forging Magical Armor

So, adventurer, you have accepted the quest.

We begin by gathering at my home, Sheshanaga in Ullur-74, nestled under the Western Ghats, on the 4th of December, to forge magical armor that can protect and bless me for the adventure to be scaled.

The Naandi Ceremony will commence around 9 AM and finish by 11 AM. Followed by the final feast before we head out on the trail.

Optional Side Quests

  • Explore the new home constructed on the Farm
  • Visit the local Deity temples, similar to the ones depicted in Kantara.

How to reach the Quest Area

So, adventurer, how do you reach the Home base? Well that is up to you, whether you choose to Fly here, use the railroad, on horseback or just snap your fingers and just apparate there from thin air, is all on you. These are some of the ways I believe you can make it there.

  • Get a Flight to Mangalore Airport (IXE), then catch a Bus to Kundapur City or Udupi City and then a Private Cab to the location from there (about 30-50 km) or get a Cab directly from the Airport to the location (about 120 km)
  • Catch a train to Kundapur Railway Station or Udupi Railway Station, then a private Cab to the location (about 25-50 km)
  • Catch a bus to Kundapur City or Udupi City, then a private Cab to the location (30 – 50km)
  • Drive yourself to the location, would not suggest a very large vehicle as you will have to travel on multiple small village roads, in slightly hilly areas.

Suggested Gear for the Quest

Any Casual, Formal or Traditional Clothes

Quest 2

The Dance Begins

Now we move on to the battle arena, or should I say the Dance Floor.

On the eve of the Challenge, 5th of December, we gather at the arena for a show of Song and Dance, with performances by family and friends. Who knows you might be called up on to show your moves also.

The Sangeet Ceremony will commence around 7 PM and finish by 10 PM. Followed by a meal to fill up our Power Levels back to the brim for the next day’s Main Event.

How to reach the Quest Area

The quest now takes us up winding ghats to the city of Shivamogga. It is about 130km from the Home base. You can use the following options to reach there.

  • Get a Flight to Shivamogga Airport (RQY), then a Private Cab to the location from there (about 10km)
  • Catch a train to Shivamogga Railway Station, then a private Cab to the location (about 1.5 km)
  • Catch a bus to Shivamogga from Bengaluru or Udupi, then a private Cab to the location ( < 5km)
  • Drive yourself to the location, very scenic routes from Udupi to Shivamogga

Suggested Gear for the Quest

Any Casual or Formal clothes.

Quest 3: CRITICAL OBJECTIVE

The Ceremony

This is the moment Namratha and I need your support and presence the most. This is the only Quest which needs to be absolutely completed from your end.

On the December of 6th, Sunday, Namratha and I will be slaying the dragon called Marriage, the Wedding Beast at Shree Matha Mangalya Mandira.

Before the showdown we will have Breakfast at 7AM followed by the ceremonies reaching the zenith at 11 AM. Then we will have the victory feast at 12:30 PM

Please do stay back until after 2PM, so that I can meet and reminisce with my loyal adventurers for showing up.

Optional Side Quests

  • Visit the famous Jog Falls
  • Visit the multiple Elephant sanctuaries or wildlife parks near by
  • Some of the best treks in Karnataka are close by here in the Western Ghats
  • Multiple Water Sport Activities around the multiple rivers originating from the Western Ghats
  • Try some of the delicious local Malnad cuisine

Quest 4: The Epilogue

The Afterparty

And now we have reached the final step of this adventure. In my hometown, Kundapur City, the city I grew up in, we will have the final party of this grand Quest of union.

After a slight respite to get back our strengths back to the optimal level, on the 9th of December, we will have the Reception Ceremony at Venkata Laxmi Gardens. The Gardens should be open around 7 PM till 11 PM in the night. There will also be one final grand feast at the venue.

At this final celebration, there will be lights, cameras, music and food. Only question that remains, will you?

Optional Side Quests

  • Visit any of the multiple beaches around the city all along the coast. (Kodi Beach, Marvanthe Beach)
  • The whole district is known for holy temples scattered around the district, including the Saptha Kshethras
  • Some of the beaches have some amazing water sport activities ranging from Snorkeling to Kayaking.
  • Try some of the delicious local coastal cuisine, like the famous Ghee Roasts at Shetty Lunch Home, or some the beach side restaurants with fresh sea food that you can probably see before you eat. Then there are multiple places to have authentic South Indian Breakfast around the city.

How to reach the Quest Area

Most of the steps to reach the location are very similar to reach my home with the only caveat being the city is far closer and has some of the best roads available in India.

  • Get a Flight to Mangalore Airport (IXM), then catch a Bus to Kundapur City, after which you can catch an Auto (about 1-3 km) or get a Cab directly from the Airport to the location (about 80 km)
  • Catch a train to Kundapur Railway Station or Udupi Railway Station, then a private Cab to the location (about 5-35 km)
  • Catch a bus to Kundapur City then a private Cab/Auto to the location (1 – 5km)
  • Drive yourself to the location, whether you are travelling from Bengaluru, or Shivamogga or somewhere closer by, these are some of the most scenic and wide roads you’d find in the country.
